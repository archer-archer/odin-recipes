Key Lime Pie

A perfect treat; this easy key lime pie is tart yet creamy.

  • Prep time: 25 mins
  • Cook time: 30 mins
  • Cool time: 8 hrs
  • Servings: 8 slices

Ingredients

Crust:

  • 1 and ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ⅓ cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter melted and slightly cooled

Filling:

  • 1 cup key lime juice
  • 2 (14-ounce) cans sweetened condensed milk
  • 5 large egg yolks

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Combine the graham cracker crumbs and sugar in a mixing bowl and mix until well combined. Add the melted butter and stir until fully combined and all of the crumbs are moistened.
  3. Scoop the mixture into a 9 inch pie plate and firmly press it down into an even layer on the bottom and up around the sides of the dish.
  4. Bake at 350°F (180°C) for 10 minutes. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool for 10 minutes while you make the filling.
  5. Keep oven temperature at 350°F (180°C).
  6. To make the key lime pie filling: Combine the key lime juice, sweetened condensed milk, and egg yolks in a large mixing bowl and whisk until fully combined.
  7. Pour the filling into the slightly cooled graham cracker crust and spread it around into one even layer.
  8. Bake for 18 to 22 minutes or until the top of the pie is set, the pie will still be jiggly.
  9. Remove from the oven and transfer to a wire rack to cool for 2 hours. Transfer to the refrigerator to chill for at least 6 hours or overnight. Once chilled, top with whipped cream and enjoy!
